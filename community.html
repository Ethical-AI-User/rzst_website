Join Our Community

Welcome! We're building a community of individuals passionate about ethical AI. We believe that collaborative, diverse perspectives are *essential* for building a future where AI benefits all of humanity.

This community is a space to:

  • Connect with educators, researchers, developers, students, and advocates.
  • Share ideas, resources, and best practices related to ethical AI.
  • Discuss challenges and solutions .
  • Contribute to a collective effort to promote responsible and equitable AI development.
  • Stay informed about the most recent efforts .

Get Involved

We believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute to this effort. Here are some ways you can get involved:

  • Share your expertise: Are you an educator, researcher, developer, or advocate with experience in ethical AI? We welcome your insights and contributions to our discussions and resources.
  • Contribute to our resource library: Do you have articles, research papers, tools, or other resources related to ethical AI? Share them with the community!
  • Volunteer your time: We're always looking for volunteers to help with tasks like moderating the forum, organizing events, and developing content.
  • Spread the word: Help us reach a wider audience by sharing our website and resources with your network.
  • Donate: If you are able to support us with a financial donation, all funds will be used to support our work.

To learn more about contributing, please contact us at contact@rzst.org.
