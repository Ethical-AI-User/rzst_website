Join Our Community

Welcome! We're building a community of individuals passionate about Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) in education and its critical connection to ethical Artificial Intelligence (AI). We believe that collaborative, diverse perspectives are *essential* for building a future where AI benefits all of humanity.

This community is a space to:

  • Connect with educators, researchers, developers, students, and advocates.
  • Share ideas, resources, and best practices related to DEI and ethical AI.
  • Discuss challenges and solutions in navigating the current landscape of DEI in education.
  • Contribute to a collective effort to promote responsible and equitable AI development.
  • Stay informed about the most recent efforts to archive, and preserve the ongoing attacks of DEI.

Get Involved

We believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute to this effort. Here are some ways you can get involved:

  • Share your expertise: Are you an educator, researcher, developer, or advocate with experience in DEI or ethical AI? We welcome your insights and contributions to our discussions and resources.
  • Contribute to our resource library: Do you have articles, research papers, tools, or other resources related to DEI in education or ethical AI? Share them with the community!
  • Volunteer your time: We're always looking for volunteers to help with tasks like moderating the forum, organizing events, and developing content.
  • Spread the word: Help us reach a wider audience by sharing our website and resources with your network.
  • Donate: If you are able to support us with a financial donation, all funds will be used in the proliferation of DEI.

To learn more about contributing, please contact us at contact@rzst.org.
