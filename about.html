About RZST

RZST is dedicated to promoting the ethical development of AI, grounded in Indigenous knowledge and data sovereignty. We envision a future where technology empowers all communities, respects cultural values, and promotes social justice.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between AI development and Indigenous perspectives, ensuring that technology aligns with the principles of respect, sovereignty, and equity. We are committed to fostering a collaborative environment where Indigenous knowledge systems directly shape the creation of responsible and beneficial AI.

Our Values

  • Respect: We honor the diverse cultures and traditions of Indigenous peoples.
  • Sovereignty: We uphold the right of Indigenous communities to self-determination and data sovereignty.
  • Collaboration: We work in genuine partnership with Indigenous communities, ensuring their voices are central and their leadership is valued.
  • Equity: We are committed to achieving social justice and equitable outcomes for all, particularly those marginalized by technological advancements.
  • Transparency: We believe in open communication and accountability in our practices and partnerships.
