Collaborative Intelligence: The Human-in-the-Loop

RZST is not an unsupervised, black-box algorithm. We recognize that true innovation requires a collaborative intelligence system that can work with human researchers rather than replacing them. Our methodology relies on an absolute division of labor. Crucially, our human operators act as the central orchestrators. While the AI generates hypotheses, human researchers provide interpretation, validation, and theoretical understanding to ensure all computational outputs align strictly with clinical and regulatory frameworks.
