Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026

This privacy policy applies to www.RZST.org, which is hosted on GitHub Pages.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Data (via GitHub Pages)

Because the Site is hosted on GitHub Pages, GitHub automatically collects standard server log information when you visit. This may include:

  • Your IP address
  • The date and time of your request
  • The type of browser and operating system you use
  • The referring web page (if any)

This information is used by GitHub to maintain the security and performance of the Site. It is not used by us to identify individual visitors. For more details, please see GitHub’s Privacy Statement at docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement.

No Direct Collection

We do not use forms, cookies, analytics tools, or any other means to collect personal information directly from you. The only way to contact us is via an email link, which opens your default email client. Any information you choose to send in that email is voluntary and is used only to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

We do not access, store, or share the server logs collected by GitHub. We do not sell or trade any information that may be contained in those logs.

Your Privacy Rights (California / US Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request information about the personal data we collect, or to request deletion of that data. Since we do not collect or store personal data ourselves, please contact us if you have concerns about information that may have been logged by GitHub.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at:

contact@rzst.org

All computational framework is designed to operate in strict compliance with de-identification protocols in respective domains.
