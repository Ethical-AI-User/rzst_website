Key Concepts

Ethical Inference

Understanding and mitigating biases in AI inference to ensure fair and equitable outcomes.

Indigenous Data Sovereignty

Recognizing the rights of Indigenous peoples to control and manage their data.

Algorithmic Justice

Promoting fairness and accountability in AI algorithms to prevent discrimination.
