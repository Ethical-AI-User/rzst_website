Ethical AI for Indigenous Sovereignty

rzst.org is dedicated to promoting the ethical development and use of Artificial Intelligence, grounded in Indigenous knowledge and data sovereignty. We believe in building a future where technology empowers all communities. Explore our resources to learn more, get involved with our community, and support our mission.

Upholding Data Sovereignty

Indigenous communities deserve to control their data, ensuring that AI development respects cultural protocols and knowledge systems.

Advancing Algorithmic Justice

We work to combat algorithmic bias and promote equitable AI systems that serve all communities, particularly those historically marginalized.

Preserving Cultural Heritage

We support the use of AI to revitalize Indigenous languages, document traditional knowledge, and safeguard cultural heritage for future generations.
