Donate Today to Support Ethical AI and DEI in Education

Your generous donations directly support our mission to promote Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) in education and ensure the ethical development and deployment of Artificial Intelligence (AI). We believe that technology should serve *all* communities, and your contribution helps us:

  • Advocate for policies that protect and expand DEI initiatives in education.
  • Develop and share resources for educators and students on ethical AI and inclusive practices.
  • Support research on the intersection of DEI and AI to mitigate bias and promote fairness.
  • Build a community of individuals committed to a just and equitable future shaped by technology.
  • Archive and preserve essential DEI-related data and resources that are currently at risk.
